About Windrow Studio

Real conversations with rising musicians. Thoughtful production built around honesty, curiosity, and craft.

Our Story

Windrow Studio was created out of a simple idea: talk to musicians on their way up and listen. No fame chasing, no industry filter, just real conversations with real artists figuring it out as they go.


It is a one person operation based in rural Indiana, built by someone who genuinely wants to know what gives these artists their drive and what they have had to overcome to keep moving forward.

How We Work

Most episodes are recorded remotely when that is the most accessible way to capture the story. There is also a dream of sitting down in person when the timing is right. The tone stays casual, friendly, and funny. Think coffee with someone who just happens to have a great story.


There is nothing to sell here. Windrow Studio exists to share what matters most. The grind. The music. The people behind it all.
