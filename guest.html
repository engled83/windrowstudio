Guest application

This is a curated remote podcast with working musicians and the people behind the scenes. If you have a real story and can show up prepared, apply below.

Apply now Watch episodes

If you want to share this link: windrowstudio.com/guest

Quick fit check
  • You can do remote video with camera on.
  • You can wear headphones and record in a quiet space.
  • You have a best live video link ready.
  • You have a story beyond “check out my song.”

If that sounds like you, apply. If not, wait until you have those pieces.

Who I’m looking for
  • Artists with momentum (shows, releases, real trajectory).
  • Producers, writers, engineers, managers, venue buyers.
  • People with a clear point of view and real stories.
Have this ready
  • Your best live performance video link.
  • Spotify, Apple Music, or credits link.
  • One clip-worthy story you can tell on camera.
Requirements
  • Remote video, camera on.
  • Headphones strongly preferred.
  • Decent audio, no loud room noise.
  • Curated show, not every application is accepted.

Apply

Fill out the form below. If it’s a fit, you’ll get an email with next steps and scheduling options. If you do not hear back, it’s not a fit right now.

What happens next
  • I review applications in batches.
  • If it’s a fit, I’ll email you with next steps and scheduling.
  • We may do a quick 10 minute pre-call to confirm fit and setup.
How to be a great guest
  • Bring one story with stakes (mistake, hard lesson, turning point).
  • Bring one practical insight people can use.
  • Be ready for clips, not just promotion.
