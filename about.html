About Windrow Studio

Windrow Studio was created out of a simple idea: talk to musicians on their way up and listen. No fame-chasing, no industry filter, just real conversations with real artists figuring it out as they go.

It's a one-man operation based in rural Indiana, built by someone who genuinely wants to know what gives these artists their drive — and what they’ve had to overcome to keep moving forward.

Most episodes will be recorded remotely, but there’s definitely a dream of sitting down in person when the timing’s right. The tone? Casual, friendly, funny — like grabbing coffee with someone who just happens to have a hell of a story.

There’s nothing to sell here, and nothing to lose. Windrow Studio exists to share the stuff that matters: the grind, the music, the people behind it all.
