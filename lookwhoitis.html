The Point

No fake polish. No empty promo talk.

The show is built around the part of music careers people usually skip over: the years before the win, the decisions that cost something, the setbacks, the lessons, and the work it takes to keep going.

For artists with a real story Not just a release to promote, but a path worth talking about.
For listeners who care about the process Music talk that goes deeper than surface-level interview answers.
For the independent music world A place for country, rock, Americana, songwriters, players, and the people behind the scenes.
