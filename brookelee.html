Brooke Lee

Look Who It Is Podcast Guest
Brooke Lee joins Dane Engle on Look Who It Is to talk about the years behind the big moments in her career. From Broadway closing shifts and early songwriting sessions to landing a national Chevrolet campaign that aired during the Olympic opening ceremonies, Brooke shares what it really took to reach this stage. She also discusses the creative vision behind her upcoming EP Desert Darling, the growth that shaped it, and her commitment to building a career focused on longevity rather than overnight success. The episode offers a candid look at process, persistence, and the realities of building something that lasts in Nashville.

About Look Who It Is

Look Who It Is is a podcast documenting how musicians actually build and sustain careers. Hosted by Dane Engle, the show focuses on honest conversations with artists, producers, and songwriters working in today’s music industry.
