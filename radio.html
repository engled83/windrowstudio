Submit Music

Independent rock, country, Americana, Red Dirt, roots, and related artists are welcome to send music for consideration.

Want your song considered?

Head over to the contact page and send your artist name, song title, streaming link, clean audio file if available, and a short note about the song. Submission does not guarantee airplay, but every good-fit submission will be reviewed.

Best fit for the station:
  • Independent rock, country, Americana, roots, Red Dirt, and singer-songwriter music.
  • Finished songs that are clean enough for radio play.
  • Artists with a real story, strong writing, or a sound that stands out.
  • No AI-generated music submissions.
