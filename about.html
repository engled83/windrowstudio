About Windrow Studio

Real conversations with rising musicians and the people behind the music. Built around honesty, curiosity, and craft.

Our Story

Windrow Studio started with a simple idea: talk to people who make music happen and listen. That includes artists on the rise, session players, songwriters, producers, engineers, managers, and anyone else who keeps this industry moving.

The show isn’t built on hype or celebrity. It’s built on real people fighting for their lane and shaping their sound day by day. No filter. No industry polish. Just honest conversations that highlight the work behind the work.

It’s a one-person operation based in rural Indiana, created by someone who genuinely wants to know what fuels these careers and what people have had to overcome to stay in the game.

How We Work

Most episodes are recorded remotely, which makes the show accessible to independent artists and busy industry professionals across the country. In-person interviews will grow over time as the studio expands and travel increases.

The tone stays casual and human. Like grabbing coffee with someone who happens to have a damn good story about the music world. No scripts. No pressure. Just a space where musicians and industry folks can talk about the grind, the challenges, the victories, and the weird little moments that shaped their careers.

Windrow Studio isn’t here to sell anything. It exists to spotlight the people who make music possible and to give listeners a real look into the world behind the sound.
