Welcome to Windrow Studio

We're a small, independent creative studio based in rural Indiana — focused on honest conversations, real stories, and helping musicians be heard. Whether it’s audio, video, or just showing up and listening, this is where it starts.

What We’re About

Windrow Studio is run by one guy with a mic, a passion for music, and a lot of questions. The goal isn’t to go viral — it’s to get real. No studio fluff, no fake hype — just genuine stories from artists who are still climbing.

Look Who It Is! Podcast

Our first podcast project is called Look who it is! — and it’s built around spotlighting musicians who haven’t “made it” yet but absolutely should. We’ll talk about their journey, their grind, and what drives them to keep going.
