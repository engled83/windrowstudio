Look Who It Is!

Conversations with rising musicians from small towns chasing big dreams. Real voices, no filters, no fluff.

Listen on Spotify

Episode 1: Jake – Gigs, Led Zeppelin & The Nude Beach Offer

Jake from Chinese Jailbreak talks about his first gig at his cousin’s 18th birthday, how Led Zeppelin shaped his sound, and the wildest offer he’s ever turned down.

Episode 2: John – Guitar Roots, Jamming, and a Keyboard Bass

John Wallace talks about the first songs he ever learned, the guitar and tuning he’s been hooked on lately, and what happened when a keyboard player took over the bass role in his latest jam sessions.

Episode 3: Kyle's Rock & Roll Chronicles – Stories from the Stage

Kyle shares stories from his journey in rock and roll, from life on stage to the music scene. Behind-the-scenes tales, laughs, and real talk from the road.

Episode 4: Dear Iris – New Album & East Texas Roots

Philip and Jerry from Dear Iris talk about their East Texas roots, their journey as a band, and what went into crafting their newest album. They share stories about writing, recording, and the inspiration behind their sound.
