Quick fit check

This is a better fit for guests who can carry a real conversation and are ready to talk about the work behind the work.

  • Remote video with camera on
  • Headphones and a quiet space
  • Links ready for music, socials, or live performance
  • A story with substance, not just promotion

What makes a strong guest

The best episodes come from people who can go deeper than the basic bio and talk honestly about the road behind the music.

Clear story

There should be something real to dig into, not just a new release to mention.

Good setup

Decent audio, stable internet, camera on, and a place quiet enough to record.

Prepared

Links, socials, music, and basic background should already be ready to send.

Right fit

Artists and music people who care about the long game usually fit the show best.
