What the station is

A simple, focused station with a clear lane: country during the day, rock at night, and independent artists mixed with familiar songs.

Positioning

Look Who It Is Radio gives listeners a clean place to hear country, rock, Americana, alternative country, roots music, and independent artists worth discovering.

Why partner

This is not a mass-market radio buy. It is a focused, affordable way to be attached to independent music, local shows, and a growing station brand.

Focused audience

Reach people already connected to country, rock, live music, artist discovery, and local/regional music culture.

Early supporter value

Founding partners can get in before the station is crowded with ads or sponsor clutter.

Cross-promotion

Promotion can include station mentions, social posts, stories, giveaways, and venue/event spotlights.

Promo options

Simple options that are realistic for a growing station. Packages can be built around station audio, social content, venue events, or giveaways.

15 or 30 second station spot

A clean sponsor message played on the station during agreed programming blocks.

Venue or event spotlight

Short mentions for upcoming shows, ticket links, venue events, or artist lineups that fit the station audience.

Sponsored feature

Sponsor a repeatable feature like Signal Boost, New Music Spotlight, or an independent artist highlight.

Social post or story

Short sponsor or venue mentions through Instagram and TikTok content tied to the station.

Giveaway partner

Ticket, merch, gift card, hat, or product giveaway tied to station and social promotion.

Website placement

Basic sponsor placement on the station site or sponsor page with a direct link.

Starter packages

Use these as clean starting points when talking to venues or sponsors. Add pricing later when you are ready.

Venue Show Push

Station mentions, a short social post or story, and a direct link to the event or ticket page.

Sponsor Founding Partner Test

A short sponsor spot, website placement, and one social mention for a simple trial campaign.

Giveaway Listener Giveaway

Partner-provided prize promoted through the station and social posts with clear winner instructions.
