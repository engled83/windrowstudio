Submit Music for Rotation Consideration

Independent rock, country, Americana, Red Dirt, roots, and related artists are welcome to send music for review.

Want your song considered?

Send your artist name, song title, streaming link, clean audio file if available, and a short note about the song. Submission does not guarantee airplay, but every good-fit submission will be reviewed.
