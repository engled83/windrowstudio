Venue & sponsor info.

Look Who It Is Radio is a licensed independent country and rock streaming station built to spotlight real artists, mix in familiar songs, and help listeners discover music worth hearing.

The format is simple: country during the day, rock at night. The station streams through Live365 and is built for listeners who want familiar songs alongside independent artists they may not hear anywhere else.

Promo options

Options can be built around the station, social posts, giveaways, event mentions, or a short campaign. No inflated promises. Just clean promotion.

Station audio spot

A short 15 or 30 second sponsor message played on the station during agreed programming blocks.

Sponsored feature

Sponsor a repeatable feature like Signal Boost, New Music Spotlight, or a weekly independent artist highlight.

Venue or event spotlight

Simple mentions for upcoming shows, venue events, ticket links, or artist lineups that fit the station audience.

Social post or story

Short sponsor or venue mentions through Instagram or TikTok content connected to the station.

Giveaway partner

Ticket, merch, hat, gift card, or product giveaway tied to station promotion and social posts.

Custom promo package

A simple combination of station mentions, social posts, and feature sponsorship based on the goal.
