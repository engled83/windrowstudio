Look who it is!

A new podcast from Windrow Studio featuring conversations with rising musicians from small towns and big dreams. Real voices. No filters. No fluff.

Episode 1: Jake – Gigs, Led Zeppelin & The Nude Beach Offer

Jake from Chinese Jailbreak talks about his first gig at his cousin’s 18th birthday, how Led Zeppelin shaped his sound, and the wildest offer he’s ever turned down.

Episode 2: John – Guitar Roots, Jamming, and a Keyboard Bass

John Wallace talks about the first songs he ever learned, the guitar and tuning he’s been hooked on lately, and what happened when a keyboard player took over the bass role in his latest jam sessions.

Episode 3: Kyle's Rock & Roll Chronicles – Stories from the Stage

Welcome to Kyle's Rock & Roll Chronicles, where the heart of music beats strong! Kyle shares captivating stories from his journey in the world of rock and roll — from life on stage to the vibrant music scene. Whether you're a die-hard fan or just curious about the life of a rock artist, this one's packed with behind-the-scenes tales, laughs, and real talk from the road.
