The Show

Not another surface-level music interview.

This show is built for conversations that go past the release cycle. It is about what artists went through, what they learned, what almost stopped them, and what kept them moving.

Artists Singers, bands, songwriters, and musicians.
Industry Managers, producers, players, and builders.
Stories The road before the audience saw the win.
Music Country, rock, Americana, roots, and independent scenes.
What It Is

A podcast about the real path behind the music.

The goal is simple: have honest conversations with people who are building something in music, whether they are on stage, behind the board, behind the scenes, or still grinding toward the next step.

For artists

A place to talk about more than the newest single. The story, the work, the bad breaks, the growth, and the moments that actually shaped the career.

For listeners

Music conversations with more weight than the usual promo cycle. Real background, real perspective, and stories that make the songs mean more.

For the music community

A bridge between podcast interviews, independent radio, local scenes, and the people trying to build something that lasts.

The Mission

No fake polish. No empty promo talk.

Look Who It Is focuses on the part of music careers people usually skip over: the years before the breakthrough, the decisions that cost something, the setbacks, the lessons, and the work it takes to keep going.

Real career stories Conversations about the path, not just the result.
Independent-minded Built around artists and music people with something real to say.
Connected to the music The podcast works alongside Look Who It Is Radio to support independent rock, country, Americana, roots, and songwriter-driven music.
Simple format Conversation first. No forced bits. No overproduced nonsense.
