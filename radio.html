Submit Music for Review

Independent rock, country, Americana, Red Dirt, roots, alternative, and related artists can submit music for airplay consideration.

Send the song, the file, and the story behind it.

Use the submission form to send your song, audio file link, release info, artist details, and background on the track. Submissions are reviewed for station fit, quality, and replay value. Submission does not guarantee airplay.

Submit Music for Review
Best fit for the station:

  • Finished songs ready for public streaming.
  • Artists with a real story, strong writing, or a sound that stands out.
  • Digital audio files are required for airplay consideration.
  • No AI-generated music submissions.
